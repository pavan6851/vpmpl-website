Vaasudeva Process Management is led by Srinivas Prasad K S, with 36+ years of hands-on industrial automation experience across water treatment, chemical, pharma, and process industries. We are not a trading company — we are a specialist engineering solutions provider.

Our focus is narrow by design: process instrumentation, PLC/SCADA systems, analyser solutions, and turnkey automation projects for water & wastewater and chemical/process industries — delivered with the discipline and quality that large EPCs expect.

  • Primary expertise: Water & Wastewater and Chemical/Process Industries

Our Company

Vaasudeva Process Management Pvt Ltd

Vaasudeva Process Management is a Bengaluru-based specialist automation engineering company founded and led by Srinivas Prasad K S, who brings 36+ years of direct industrial automation experience across water treatment, chemical manufacturing, pharma, and process industries. Unlike large system integrators where your project is handled by a junior team, at VPMPL you work with the founder and senior engineers on every engagement.

Our deliberate focus on Water & Wastewater and Chemical/Process Industries means our engineers have accumulated genuinely deep application knowledge — the kind that prevents costly mistakes during design, saves time during commissioning, and earns client confidence on every project.
