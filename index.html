Who We Are

Built on Engineering Precision

Vaasudeva Process Management Pvt Ltd (VPMPL) is a premier industrial engineering services company headquartered in India, with a global footprint spanning petrochemical, energy, pharmaceutical, and manufacturing sectors.

We combine deep domain expertise with cutting-edge digital technology to deliver end-to-end process automation, instrumentation engineering, and industrial digitalization solutions that drive real operational outcomes.

  • End-to-end EPC and EPCM capabilities for industrial projects
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management systems
  • Industry 4.0 and digital twin implementation expertise
  • OT Cybersecurity and IEC 62443 compliance

Our Company

Vaasudeva Process Management Pvt Ltd

Founded in 2006, VPMPL has grown from a specialist instrumentation consultancy into a full-spectrum industrial engineering services company. With offices across India and project delivery experience spanning the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Africa, we bring global perspective to local engineering challenges.

Our team of 85+ engineers brings domain depth across process automation, control system design, SCADA, and industrial digitalization — covering the entire plant lifecycle from conceptual design to decommissioning support.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2006
Company Founded
VPMPL established in Bengaluru as an instrumentation engineering consultancy, serving chemical and petrochemical clients in South India.
2009
First DCS Project
Completed first large-scale DCS migration project for a major refinery in Gujarat, expanding service portfolio to include control system engineering.
2012
ISO 9001 Certification
Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification, formalizing quality management systems and opening doors to international project opportunities.
2015
Middle East Expansion
First international project delivery in UAE and Oman, establishing VPMPL's credentials as a global industrial engineering partner.
2018
Digital Transformation Practice
Launched dedicated Industry 4.0 and IIoT practice, delivering first digital twin implementation for a polymer manufacturing plant.
2021
OT Cybersecurity Division
Established OT security practice aligned to IEC 62443, helping clients protect critical infrastructure from evolving cyber threats.
2024
350+ Projects Milestone
Crossed the 350-project milestone with 85+ engineers, 120+ active clients, and project delivery across 12 countries.
